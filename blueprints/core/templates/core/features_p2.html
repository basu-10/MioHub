MioSpace — Features

Notes, folders and collaborative whiteboards — organised around you and your teams.

Hierarchical folders

Nested folders with breadcrumbs make it easy to organise large document collections.

MioWord (documents)

Rich note editor with autosave, versioning hints and markdown support for fast writing.

MioBoard (whiteboards)

Canvas-style boards for sketches and diagrams — saved as structured JSON for edit/replay.

Sharing & permissions

Share notes or folders with users, with per-item visibility flags and simple access control.
